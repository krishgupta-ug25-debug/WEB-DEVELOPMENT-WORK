NESCAFE AT NSUT

A Popular Hangout Spot for Students

The Nescafe outlet at NSUT is a popular spot among students for quick snacks, chilled beverages, and coffee during breaks. After reopening recently, it's become a hangout space where students refuel for their studies and campus activities. The outlet is known for its casual vibe and its role as a social hub for friends to catch up over coffee and chat about campus life.

Key features of Nescafe at NSUT:

Offers a variety of coffee, teas, cold beverages, and light snacks. Conveniently located so students can easily grab something between classes. A comeback that's been much anticipated after periods of closure, generating excitement within the student community. Students mention it as a spot where campus life feels lively, especially during events or stressful exam periods.
