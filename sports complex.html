SPORTS COMPLEX

NSUT has a well-equipped sports complex that caters to the diverse interests of its students. The complex includes facilities for various sports such as basketball and volleyball. There are also outdoor fields for cricket, football, and athletics. The sports complex is designed to promote physical fitness and encourage students to participate in extracurricular activities. Regular sports events and tournaments are organized to foster a spirit of competition and camaraderie among students.

VARIETY OF SPORTS

  • Volleyball
  • Cricket
  • Football
  • Athletics
