Netaji Subhas University of Technology (NSUT), formerly NSIT, is a leading state university located in Dwarka, Delhi, known for excellence in technical education and research. It was established in 1983 as Delhi Institute of Technology, gained university status in 2018, and is recognized by UGC and AICTE.

Key Features
  • NSUT offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and PhD programs in engineering, management, sciences, and humanities.
  • Admissions are based on top entrance exams like JEE Main (B.Tech), GATE (M.Tech), CAT/MAT (MBA), and university-conducted tests (Ph.D).
  • The campus spans approximately 145 acres and is fully residential, with modern infrastructure, hostels, sports facilities, and research centers
  • Ranked among the top engineering institutions in India, with notable alumni in academia, industry, and entertainment.
  • The median placement package in 2023 was around INR 17 LPA, indicating strong industry connections and career prospects.
